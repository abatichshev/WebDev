Recipe #1: Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Ground beef 500g
Tomato sauce 300ml
Onion 1 (chopped)
Garlic 2 cloves (minced)
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Salt & Pepper To taste

Steps:

Step 1: Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant.

Step 3: Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 4: Pour in the tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 5: Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Garnish with fresh basil or Parmesan cheese if desired.
